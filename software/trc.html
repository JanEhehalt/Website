Current state

"The Restless Caverns" is considered a finished game. We have published a version in the Google PlayStore . This version has problems with its controls, which will be fixed in the future as soon as we are in the mood and have the time to do it. Also there are missing textures on the sixth and seventh floor. But in general the game is playable and you can beat it.

About

"The Restless Caverns" is a dungeon crawler, which was developed by Jonathan Hager and myself. We developed it as our first game within the LibGDX engine. The goal of the game is it to beat all seven floors. On every floor you have to find the key in order to be able to reach the next floor.
